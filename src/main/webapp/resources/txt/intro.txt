More than 10 years ago, the Rottenland Empire invaded the kingdom of
Edenia. Since then, people have periodically tried to escape the war
to the kingdom of Freedomland. To do this, they turn to guides who accompany
them almost to the borders of Edenia. Then the travelers must overcome the
rest of the way on their own. Today this mission has fallen to You,
Traveler. So we are now in the mountains of Edenia, about 3 kilometers from
the border with Freedomland...
